# PSU_Kibana
<h1>Kibana (Ransomware Event Analysis)</h1>

<h2>Description</h2>
During my capstone project, my group divided the different areas of analysis amongst ourselves, and I was in charge of the network analysis. After trying to open the network information in Wireshark, I discovered that the data given to us was only interpretable within Kibana. The key takeaway from this project was discovering various C2 events and a data exfiltration event. My discoveries played a critical role in our group's analysis, and I presented them to stakeholders in digestible terms they could understand. Comparing C2 to a thief waiting inside a vault for a signal during a bank heist is easier to comprehend than going on about a huge volume of HTTP traffic being sent to an external IP (216.74.105.201) over a very short window over port 80.
